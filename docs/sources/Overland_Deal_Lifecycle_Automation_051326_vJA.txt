ONE-TEAM · A CORE VALUE, NOT YET A TECHNOLOGICAL REALITY

One Team is a core CB value, but it is not yet a technological reality -- the data across our strategies does not talk to itself. When an IP in any strategy isn't sought out, forgets a detail, or leaves the platform, their insights
leave with them. Build the controls above on a common architecture and the knowledge base does not just persist -- it grows, compounds across Credit, PE, and RE, and accrues to every future deal team.

These controls are not contingent on hitting 225 names -- they are the operating standard for an institutionally managed direct lending GP, and the platform that delivers them is
portable across Centerbridge. Several gaps (IC approval tracking, valuation, compliance certs) are audit and regulatory exposures today per Section V -- automating them reduces error
risk and incremental compliance cost as the portfolio scales, but the obligation exists independent of growth path.
